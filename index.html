About Cinema

Cinema History 

History of Cinema is a 15-episode documentary series that explores the history of cinema. It is available on YouTube and Spotify. Each episode focuses on a different era or genre of cinema, and examines the key events and figures that shaped it. The series is hosted by film historian and critic, David Thomson.
